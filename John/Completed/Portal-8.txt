Title: Portal

Rating: 8/10

Ownership: Steam - PC

Hours Played: 2-4 Hours

Start Date: 1/1/15

Finish Date: 1/10/15

Thoughts:
So simple yet so complex. Using a portal system to solve puzzles is an 
elegant system and makes this game one of the greatest puzzle games ever created. 
On top of the excellent game mechanics the witty dialogue is always charming 
to listen too. (Especially the song at the end)
